French Toast

Enjoy a quick and satisfying breakfast with this easy French Toast recipe.Whisk eggs, sugar, salt, and milk to create a flavorful batter. Dip bread slices in the mixture and fry until golden-brown in buttered pan. Serve hot with honey or fruit for a delicious morning treat that's both crunchy and softâ€”a classic favorite for busy mornings and a delightful start to any day.

French Toast Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • Dash of salt
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • Bread slices

Instructions:

  1. Whisk together eggs, sugar, salt, and milk in a bowl until well combined.
  2. Dip bread slices into the egg mixture, coating both sides.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and add a small amount of butter.
  4. Cook each bread slice until golden-brown on both sides, flipping as needed.
  5. Remove from pan and serve hot, optionally with honey or fruit(I prefer nutella).
