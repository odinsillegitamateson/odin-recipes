Classic Milkshake

Indulge in a creamy and delicious classic milkshake. This simple recipe combines the richness of ice cream with the refreshing taste of milk. Perfect for a quick treat or dessert!

Milkshake Ingredients

  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Whipped cream (used in the image but optional)
  • Chocolate syrup (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine vanilla ice cream, milk, and vanilla extract.
  2. Blend on high speed until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into a chilled glass.
  4. If desired, top with whipped cream and drizzle with chocolate syrup.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!
